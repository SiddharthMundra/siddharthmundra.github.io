1. spoilerdetector — automatic spoiler detection in book reviews

as someone who loves reading, i get frustrated when a book review accidentally includes a spoiler. i developed spoilerdetector to automatically identify and filter spoilers in book reviews. read the paper. github.

  • used the goodreads book reviews dataset with 2.5m+ annotated reviews.
  • implemented keyword, bag-of-words, and logistic regression baselines.
  • developed a distilbert transformer model with regularization and class balancing.
  • evaluated with precision, fpr, fnr, and roc auc.

4. neural composer — symbolic music generation

machine learning

a transformer decoder trained on the nottingham midi dataset to generate folk melodies from scratch.

  • tokenized midi with prettymidi into note-on/off, velocity, and time-shift events.
  • 6-layer transformer decoder (512-dim, 8 heads, rotary embeddings) via x-transformers.
  • achieved ~95% train and ~90% validation token-level accuracy.
  • generated new melodies with nucleus sampling (top-p, temperature=0.9).
